Project Overview

Revolutionizing oral healthcare through intelligent design and technology integration

Background

With the increasing awareness of oral health and rapid technological advancement, traditional toothbrushes are no longer meeting modern consumer needs. This project addresses the gap between user expectations and current market offerings through innovative smart toothbrush design.

Objective

Design an intelligent toothbrush that combines artificial intelligence, IoT connectivity, and ergonomic principles to provide personalized oral care, real-time health monitoring, and an enhanced user experience through data-driven insights.

User Research & Analysis

Data-driven design decisions based on Kano Model analysis
