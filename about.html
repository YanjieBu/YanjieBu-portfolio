A Glimpse of Me

I'm an interaction design student currently studying at UQ, with a background in industrial design. I enjoy combining visual thinking with code to create thoughtful and engaging digital experiences.

I'm curious about how people interact with technology, and how design can make everyday moments smoother, clearer, and more delightful. I like turning abstract ideas into simple, intuitive interfaces that feel good to use.

Beyond coursework, I enjoy photography and drawing — both help me notice small things in life. I also love music, often part of my creative process. These habits keep me inspired, grounded, and connected.

I see design as a way to express emotion and care—and I'm always excited to keep learning and creating.
