Tartine Bread

A deliciously crusty sourdough bread that requires minimal effort to make.

Ingredients

  • 350g Flour
  • 100g Active Sourdough
  • 1 Teaspoon Salt
  • 50g Whole Wheat Flour

Steps

Step 1: Mix Ingredients

Combine 350g of flour, 100g of sourdough starter, and 250ml of water.

Step 2: Let it Rest

Cover the dough and let it rest for 4-6 hours.
